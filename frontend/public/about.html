Welcome to the MindMaze!

Our Quiz App is designed to help you test your knowledge across various domains, including General Knowledge, Coding, History, and more. Whether you're preparing for an exam, looking to learn new things, or just want to have some fun, this app is for you!

Features

  • Multiple quiz domains to choose from.
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation.
  • Track your progress and scores over time.
  • Retry quizzes and challenge yourself!

How to Use

Simply select a domain from the welcome page, answer the questions presented, and submit your answers to see your score. You can also access your dashboard to view your quiz statistics and progress.
